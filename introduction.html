Ancient Chinese articles of clothing refers to all kinds of clothes, hats, shoes and stockings in ancient China. They form a series of their own in the world. Their structure and style gradually change with the development of production and lifestyle. Through the study of ancient clothing, we can know the style and features of the characters in the past dynasties. In the identification of cultural relics, clothing is also an important measure of dating. There are few ancient costumes in the world. In addition to the physical objects, the figures in ancient sculptures and paintings are often important reference materials.
